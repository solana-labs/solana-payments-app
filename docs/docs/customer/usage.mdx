In this guide, you'll find all the necessary information to navigate through the payment process using Solana Pay.

## Using Solana Pay

1. **Choose Solana Pay**: On the checkout page, select Solana Pay as your payment method.

2. **Connect Wallet**: You'll be automatically prompted to connect your Solana wallet.

3. **Approve Payment**: In the Solana Pay interface, confirm the transaction.

4. **Confirm Payment**: Check the transaction history in your wallet for confirmation.

### Troubleshooting

-   **Failed Payments**: Error messages will be provided for issues such as insufficient funds, cancellations, or technical problems.

-   **Refunds**: Contact the merchant directly for refunds.

-   **Support**: For assistance, email product@solana.com.

### Additional Info

-   **Fees**: Transactions have minimal fees (less than $0.001).

-   **Product Availability**: Availability of Solana Pay depends on the merchant's settings.
